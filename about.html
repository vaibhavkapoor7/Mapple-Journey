About

Maple Journey

Maple Journey is a storytelling and resource hub that helps immigrants in Canada adjust, connect, and thrive through practical guides and real lived experiences.

Project Background & Mission

Born from our own newcomer journeys, Maple Journey brings together clear resources, peer stories, and community support. Our mission is to make settling in Canada easier turning questions into steps and steps into confidence.

Why This Site Exists

Moving countries is exciting—and overwhelming. We centralize what newcomers need: checklists, how-tos, and real stories, so adjusting to life in Canada feels guided, informed, and welcoming.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity - every background, every story, every voice.
  • Support - practical help, peer care, and clear guidance.
  • Storytelling - lived experiences that inform and inspire.
